About

Since this March Can Yaman started a new project for helping children with cancer in Italy offering free treatment for everyone.This initiative aims to raise funds in favor of the Institute of Child Neuropsychiatry of the Umberto I General Hospital in Rome. The Italian tour is scheduled for May in Puglia, Can Yaman will be in Altamura on the 11th, Corato on the 12th and Polignano a Mare on the 13th.
